I received my B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees from Sun Yat-sen University, where I was supervised by [Prof. Gang Chen](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn/teacher/Chengang).
After graduation, I worked as a Research Assistant at CAIR under the supervision of [Prof. Kang Zhou](https://charleskangzhou.github.io/) and [Prof. Gaofeng Meng](https://people.ucas.ac.cn/~gfmeng).
My research experience spans computer vision tasks (e.g., detection and segmentation), generative models such as diffusion, and medical ultrasound image analysis.
Recently, I have become increasingly interested in multimodal representation learning and vision–language models.

I am still learning and exploring these directions, and I am currently looking for new PhD or RA opportunities.
