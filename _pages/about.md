I received my B.Eng. and M.Eng. degrees from Sun Yat-sen University, where I was supervised by [Prof. Gang Chen](https://cse.sysu.edu.cn/teacher/Chengang).
After graduation, I worked as a Research Assistant at CAIR under the supervision of [Prof. Kang Zhou](https://charleskangzhou.github.io/) and [Prof. Gaofeng Meng](https://people.ucas.ac.cn/~gfmeng).

My research experience spans several areas of computer vision, including detection and segmentation, generative models such as diffusion, and medical ultrasound image analysis. Through these experiences, I have developed a growing interest in representation learning and the broader question of how models learn meaningful and robust representations.

Recently, I have become particularly interested in __multimodal representation learning and vision–language models.__ *I hope to gradually discover research problems that truly fascinate me and pursue them with patience and depth. In the long term, I aim to refine my research taste, strengthen my theoretical foundation, and develop a deeper and more independent understanding of the problems I study.*
